## Market Context

Clips targets traders in the **Solana memecoin space** who want to create professional video content from their trades. The keyword strategy focuses on three phases:

1. **Awareness:** Traders discovering trade recording/replay tools
2. **Consideration:** Comparing chart replay solutions
3. **Conversion:** Features and pricing-specific keywords

## Conclusion

Clips should focus on **high-intent keywords** in the "trade recording/proof" space while building brand awareness through "memecoin trading" education. The keyword strategy emphasizes **long-tail, question-based queries** initially, then expands to primary keywords as content authority grows.

**Estimated Organic Traffic Potential (Year 1):** 5,000-10,000 monthly organic visits
**Estimated Lead Volume:** 50-150 free trial signups/month
**Primary Conversion Path:** Keyword → Blog → Landing → Signup

The leaderboard and trader profiles represent **significant long-tail SEO opportunities** that should be prioritized for organic discovery.
